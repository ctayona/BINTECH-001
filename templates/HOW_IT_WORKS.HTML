How It Works

Start earning rewards in three simple steps

BinTECH is an intelligent waste segregation system designed to encourage responsible disposal habits on campus. By properly sorting waste using the smart kiosks, students earn EcoPoints that can be redeemed for useful campus rewards.

1

Register & Get QR Code

Create your BinTECH account using your university email. Once registered, the system generates a unique QR code linked to your account. This QR code will be used to identify you when using the smart waste sorting kiosk.

Students can access their QR code through the BinTECH web platform or mobile interface.

2

Sort Your Waste

Approach any BinTECH smart waste sorter kiosk located around the campus. Scan your QR code at the kiosk and deposit your waste in the correct category. The system detects and records your disposal activity and verifies that the waste was sorted properly.

Waste Categories:

  • • Plastic
  • • Paper
  • • Metal
  • • Organic waste
3

Earn & Redeem EcoPoints

Every correct waste disposal earns you EcoPoints. These points are stored in your BinTECH account and can be used to redeem rewards from the Rewards Page.

Popular Rewards:

  • • Free snacks and drinks vouchers
  • • Cafeteria discounts
  • • School supplies vouchers
  • • UMak WiFi access vouchers

How the BinTECH Flow Works

The platform records your sorting session when you scan your QR code at the kiosk, sorts the materials, and updates your points automatically.
